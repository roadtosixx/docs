## Configuration

Go into **addons/jobcalls/config.lua** to set which job has which job number, setup number + needjob.

You can also configure other things, add jobs or use our marker system (`useMarker = true`).

## Control Center

The control center can take over one person or several in our **service app**.

<Warning>
  It is not possible that everyone in the job is simply called, the control center must be taken over!
</Warning>
