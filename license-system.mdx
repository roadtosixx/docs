<Accordion title="I can't find my resource" icon="help-circle">
  You likely purchased the asset with the wrong **CFX.re account**.

  ### How to Transfer Your Asset

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Locate Transfer Option">
      Click **Transfer** on your asset in the cfx.re Portal
    </Step>

    <Step title="Enter Destination">
      Enter the destination username for the correct account
    </Step>

    <Step title="Confirm Transfer">
      Complete the transfer process
    </Step>
  </Steps>

  <Error>
    **Critical Warning: Irreversible Action**

    Transferring an asset (script) removes its grant from your current account. **All asset transfers are irreversible and cannot be undone.**

    If you transfer your script to another account, there is no way to get it back. We cannot help you recover it afterward.
  </Error>

## Important Notes

<Note>
  Always double-check the destination account before transferring. Once transferred, the asset cannot be returned to your original account.
</Note>

<Tip>
  If you're unsure about your CFX.re account, check your email for purchase confirmations or contact CFX.re support before attempting a transfer.
</Tip>
